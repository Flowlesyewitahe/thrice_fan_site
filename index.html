Thrice History

Thrice are a band formed in 1998 from Irvine, California. They have been associated with every subgenre of rock besides classic and hard rock. It has been composed of Dustin Kensrue on Rhythm Guitar and Vocals, Riley Breckenridge on Drums, Teppei Teranishi on lead guitar and keys, and Eddie Breckenridge on bass and backing vocals since it's inception in 1998. They have released a total of 14 albums; 11 studio albums, 2 live albums, and 1 compilation album.
