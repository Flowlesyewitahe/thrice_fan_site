Dustin Michael Kensrue

Dustin M. Kensrue was born November 18, 1980 in Orange County, California. He is the lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist of Thrice, and a solo artist. He was formerly a worship leader, and has a tattoo of Proverbs 9:10 written on his left forearm in Hebrew.

Riley Breckenridge

Riley Breckenridge was born January 5, 1975 in California. He is the drummer of Thrice. He uses Zildjian cymbals, Vic Firth sticks, Remo drumheads, and Drum Workshop hardware and pedals exclusively. He is an avid baseball fan. He has one child with his wife, Jennifer Shaw.

Edward "Eddie" Breckenridge

The younger brother of Riley Breckenridge, he plays bass and does backing screams for Thrice. Less is known about his personal life, apart from an elbow problem preventing him from turning his left hand over completely, his birthdate of November 15, 1979, and his former status as a touring bassist for Angels and Airwaves.

Teppei Teranishi

Teppei Teranishi is the lead guitarist and keyboardist of Thrice. He was born 13 September, 1980 in California. He is a craftsman of leather, and Thrice's producer.
